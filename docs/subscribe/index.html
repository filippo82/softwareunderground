Subscriptions

Donate to the Software Underground and help us spread geoscience and computery goodness.

Monthly subscriptions

Purchasing a subscription helps the Software Underground achieve its goals of spreading excellence in subsurface computing. Swung’s expenses include its Slack subscription, its web hosting fees, and supporting local meetups. With your help, we can do even more.

Annual subscriptions

If you prefer, you can also pay for a subscription annually. This will make an annual donation. If you want to make a one-time donation, please visit the donations page.

Other options

If you prefer, you can make a one-time donation or you can buy merchandise in the store.

Finally, we are looking for Partner sponsors. Visit Subscriptions, or get in touch with us.
